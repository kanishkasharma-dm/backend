    CAN'T FIND RULES? USE DIRECT URL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHOD 1: Direct URL (EASIEST!)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Copy and paste this in your browser:

https://us-east-1.console.aws.amazon.com/iot/home?region=us-east-1#/rules

This takes you directly to Rules page!

METHOD 2: AWS Console Search
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click search bar at top (next to AWS logo)
2. Type: "IoT Rules"
3. Click on "Rules (IoT Core)"

METHOD 3: Under Message Routing
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click "Message routing" (under "Manage")
2. Look for "Rules" tab or section
3. Click it
